Kaktovik


What is Kaktovik?

Kaktovik is a small town located in North Slope Borough, Alaska. It is one of the most north and isolated points in the entire state. Kaktovik's population at the 2010 census was 239.


What's so special about this place?

Kaktovik is so special because it is one of the most isolated points in Alaska. If you want to live off the grid, heres the spot. This is an ideal place for John Mooney because he wants to live off the grid. But this place isn't too off the grid. There is still power, phone service, and businesses.
